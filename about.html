Our Story

Guided by excellence, powered by technology. We're on a mission to light up homes across the nation with elegance and ease.

Lighting that lives with you.

Lumiere was founded in 2018 with a simple realization: holiday lighting shouldn't be a chore, and architectural beauty shouldn't be reserved only for the elite.

We set out to create a system that was virtually invisible during the day but capable of professional-grade light shows by night.

Quality is our North Star

Unlike temporary lights that fail after a single season, our permanent LED systems are engineered for longevity. We use aircraft-grade aluminum tracks and UV-resistant LED housings that withstand the harshest elements.
